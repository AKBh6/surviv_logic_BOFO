Colony Survival Simulation is a system-driven survival management game built in Python. The player oversees a struggling colony operating under limited resources and escalating environmental pressure. Each in-game day introduces new challenges that test the sustainability of the colony’s internal systems.

The objective is to maintain population stability, manage resources effectively, and survive long enough for the colony to stabilize.
The colony operates on a daily cycle:

Citizens consume food and energy.

Resources are produced based on population capacity.

Random events affect morale, food supply, or infrastructure.

Difficulty gradually increases over time.

Each citizen has individual health and morale attributes. Poor management leads to starvation, unrest, and eventual collapse.

The game ends when:

The colony becomes extinct, or

The colony survives a predefined number of days and stabilizes.
